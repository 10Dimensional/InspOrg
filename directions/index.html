Print Directions

From 1-15 South

Take I-15 South to the St. Rose Parkway Exit and turn Left going East.
Go 2.5 miles to Executive Airport Drive and turn right going South.
Go 2 miles on Executive Airport Drive and it will transition into Bicentennial Parkway.
Proceed on Bicentennial Parkway for .25 miles and turn right into The Overlook (Visitor Center).

To KB Home Model Center

From Bicentennial, turn Left on Via Firenze,
Left on Paladi,
Right on Borgaro to KB Home sales office.

To Toll Brothers Model Center

From Bicentennial, Turn North on Via Firenze.
Turn Left (West) on Via Festiva, continue as Via Festiva becomes Mantua Village Ave.
Follow Mantua Village, turn Left (South) on Via Delle Arti Street.
Sales Center is on your left (3177 Mantua Village Ave, Henderson, NV 89044).
