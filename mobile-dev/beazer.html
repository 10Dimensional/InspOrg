Coming 2015

Beazer Homes
2470 Paseo Verde Parkway
Suite 135
Henderson, NV 89074
(702) 837-2100

When Beazer Homes comes to Inspirada, the company will be bringing with it more than 35 years of experience building homes in the U.S., however, the company’s history of excellence dates back to the 1600s with an English builder named George Beazer. A family enterprise grown for generations from talent and innovation, Beazer Homes is one of the country’s 10 largest single-family homebuilders with more than 130,000 homes to its credit. All Beazer homes meet or exceed the benchmark for energy-efficient home construction as established by ENERGY STAR® and are designed with choice plan options to meet the personal preferences and lifestyles of its buyers. In addition, Beazer Homes is committed to providing a range of preferred lender choices to facilitate transparent competition between lenders and enhanced customer service. At Inspirada, Beazer Homes will carry on a long-held company tradition of building a better buying experience and a creating a community of quality homes that will leave its own legacy.
