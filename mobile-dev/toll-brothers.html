Since 1967, Toll Brothers has been America’s Luxury Homebuilder®. The company prides itself on building more than homes; it builds communities. Earning three of the most coveted awards in the industry—America’s Best Builder, two-time Builder of the Year by Professional Builder magazine, and the National Housing Quality Award—Toll Brothers is not only an esteemed national builder, but also a celebrated company. It ranked #1 in 2011 and 2010 in financial soundness, long-term investment and quality of products/services in the annual FORTUNE Magazine’s Most Admired Companies survey. Each Toll Brothers home offers a combination of quality materials and superior design, where every detail is meticulously crafted, and every enhancement you select is seamlessly added to create a dream home that is uniquely yours. Toll Brothers homes speak to a life of active elegance. In keeping with the Inspirada builder’s philosophy, each Toll Brothers home is designed with the highest energy-efficiency standards and keen sensitivity toward preserving the natural beauty of Nevada.
